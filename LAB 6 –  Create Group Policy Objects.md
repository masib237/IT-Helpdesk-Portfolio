# LAB  Objective
To configure and apply Group Policy settings to Organizational units.

## Steps Performed
- Created GPOs
- Applied restrictions to users
- Linked GPOs to OUs
- Forced policy updates using gpupdate

## Why This Matters
GPOs enforce security and configuration policies across an organization.

## Outcome
Policies successfully applied to domain users.
